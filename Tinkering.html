Bokken Restoration

Published: November 6, 2025
I did this once before, with a much nicer Wooden Training Katana (Bokken is their proper name I guess) that existed at my parents for years. There was some calligraphy ink in my house, and I got the idea that it could be used as a wood stain, (lo and behold, it really was back in the day!) and with it I could swap the colors on the Bokken by staining the recessed wood before sanding off the paint. It worked great! I sold it on eBay and didn't record the process. So I bought a similar one and started again!
